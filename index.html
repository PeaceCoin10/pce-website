About PCE

The ultimate goal of the PCE project is to become the core pillar of the global anti-war and peace movement, combining anti-war actions with economic incentives through the power of cryptocurrency. Our specific goals are as follows:

Promote Global Anti-War Action

Through the widespread circulation of PCE tokens, we aim to raise global awareness about the need for peace. With a decentralized blockchain system, every token holder can be a part of the global anti-war movement and contribute to the cause of peace.

Provide Economic Incentives for Anti-War Advocates

PCE is not just a symbol of peace; it is a fully developed economic platform. Through USDT positioning and PEPE mining pool rewards, advocates can participate in the global peace movement and receive substantial economic returns. This will motivate more people to join the anti-war cause, turning it into a concrete economic action, not just a slogan.

Build a Long-Term Sustainable Community Economy

Through smart contracts, mining pool rewards, and token appreciation mechanisms, PCE tokens will ensure long-term, stable growth. With a decentralized reward system, we aim to keep the value of the token and community participation stable and sustainable, providing continuous support for the global anti-war movement.

Our Partners

Tether (USDT)

PCE has formed a strategic partnership with the leading stablecoin, Tether (USDT). This partnership offers users stable and secure staking opportunities to ensure the stability of the PCE project.

PEPE Mining Pool

PEPE Mining Pool is a core partner providing mining pool rewards. By staking USDT, users will receive PCE tokens, further promoting token circulation and market recognition.
