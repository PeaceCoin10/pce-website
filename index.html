Our Partners

Tether (USDT)

PCE has formed a strategic partnership with the leading stablecoin, Tether (USDT). This partnership offers users stable and secure staking opportunities to ensure the stability of the PCE project.

PEPE Mining Pool

PEPE Mining Pool is a core partner providing mining pool rewards. By staking USDT, users will receive PCE tokens, further promoting token circulation and market recognition.

Additional Partnerships

  • Decentralized Exchanges (DEX): Increasing liquidity and trading volume.

Smart Contract Development

  • Token Issuance and Management: Smart contracts will handle PCE token supply, transfers, and transaction records.
