About PCE

PCE's ultimate goal is to become the central pillar of the global anti-war and peace movement, linking anti-war actions with economic incentives through cryptocurrency. Our specific goals are:

  • Promoting Global Anti-War Actions: By spreading PCE tokens, we aim to raise awareness about the need for peace globally. Through a decentralized blockchain system, every token holder can become a part of the global anti-war movement and contribute to the cause of peace.
  • Providing Economic Incentives to Peace Advocates: PCE is not just a symbol of anti-war; it is a mature economic platform. With the USDT staking and PEPE mining pool rewards system, advocates can participate in the global peace movement and receive substantial economic returns.
  • Building a Long-Term Sustainable Community Economy: With smart contracts, mining pool rewards, and token appreciation mechanisms, PCE tokens will ensure long-term and stable growth. A decentralized reward system will ensure the value of the token and community participation remain stable and sustainable, providing continuous support to the global anti-war movement.
